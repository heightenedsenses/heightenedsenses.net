What We Do For The Government
Heightened Senses provides federal, defense, and public safety agencies with NDAA-compliant UAS platforms, Counter-UAS sensing systems, and aerial data services — integrated and fielded by operators with real intelligence and mission backgrounds.
We Have Reviewed the Government's Needs — Here Is How We Meet Them
Government Need
NDAA-Compliant UAS Procurement Before the January 2027 Deadline
How We Meet It
We source and integrate Blue UAS-cleared, NDAA-compliant platforms — including the Red Cat Black Widow, Trichon, and FANG FPV systems — and manage the full procurement and transition process for agencies moving off prohibited technology.
Why We Can Deliver
Authorized Red Cat Holdings reseller. NDAA-compliant supply chain across airframes, avionics, power, and communications. SBA-certified SDVOSB with active SAM registration.
Government Need
Persistent Situational Awareness Without Stood-Up Programs
How We Meet It
We deploy integrated UAS and C-UAS sensing packages — aerial ISR, EO/IR payloads, radar, and secure mesh communications — as ready-to-operate mission packages for law enforcement, tribal authorities, corrections, and critical infrastructure operators.
Why We Can Deliver
Founded by a USMC CI/HUMINT GySgt. We build and operate these systems — we don't resell spec sheets. Authorized MatrixSpace C-UAS radar and Doodle Labs mesh radio reseller.
