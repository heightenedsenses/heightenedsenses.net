What We Do For The Government

Heightened Senses provides federal, defense, and public safety agencies with NDAA-compliant UAS platforms, counter-UAS detection systems, and aerial data services, integrated and fielded by operators with real intelligence and mission backgrounds. Founded and led by a USMC Gunnery Sergeant (Ret.), Purple Heart recipient, with a counterintelligence and human intelligence operational background.

Government Needs. How We Meet Them
Government Need
NDAA-Compliant UAS Procurement Before the January 2027 Deadline

How We Meet It · We source and integrate Blue UAS Cleared, NDAA-compliant platforms, including the Red Cat Black Widow, Trichon, and FANG FPV systems, and manage the full procurement and transition process for agencies moving off prohibited technology.

Why We Deliver · Authorized Red Cat Holdings reseller. NDAA-compliant supply chain across airframes, avionics, ground control, and power. SBA-Certified SDVOSB with active SAM registration.

Government Need
Airspace Awareness and C-UAS Detection Under Current Federal Authorities

How We Meet It · We design, install, maintain, and train on layered passive detection architectures: RF spectrum sensing, compact radar, EO/IR cuing, and Remote ID monitoring, unified into a common operating picture. Passive detection is deployable by state, local, tribal, and territorial agencies today and aligns with federal grant programs supporting drone detection acquisition.

Why We Deliver · Founded by a USMC counterintelligence and HUMINT specialist. Detection systems delivered through executed OEM channels. Program design, policy and OPLAN support, and proficiency training support for agencies building C-UAS programs. Priority mission sets: corrections, critical infrastructure, and public safety.

Government Need
Recurring Aerial Inspection and Infrastructure Data Deliverables

How We Meet It · We provide FAA Part 107 aerial data collection, photogrammetry, orthomosaic mapping, and digital twin generation as recurring contracted services for utilities, pipelines, transportation assets, and federal facilities.

Why We Deliver · FAA Part 107 certified. Registered on the Disaster Response Registry. NAICS 541715 and 541330 qualified. Veteran operator team.
