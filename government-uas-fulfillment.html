Process
How ItWorks
Step 01
Government Nominates Parts

The Government nominates specific components for NDAA compliance review. Heightened Senses conducts a preliminary screening and, upon authorization, prepares a full compliance evidence package per PMA-263 guidance for Government validation.

Step 02
Government Validates

The compliance documentation package is uploaded to the SRA Portal by Heightened Senses personnel using DoD-approved ECA credentials. PMA-263 serves as the final validation authority. Heightened Senses lists the component for ordering only upon official COR notification of approval.

Step 03
Government Orders

Approved components are available in this catalog at publicly listed prices. Orders are fulfilled directly to Government end users. Monthly consolidated invoicing is submitted through WAWF. Returns and RMA support handled within one business day.

Security & Compliance
Security &Compliance

Platform Security Standards

  • TLS 1.2 or higher encryption in transit for all platform communications
  • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) required for all registered Government accounts
  • Role-based access control limiting visibility to authorized users only
  • Documented vulnerability management and incident response procedures
  • Audit-ready order and delivery records maintained for all transactions

NDAA Compliance Framework

  • All components listed only after Government nomination and PMA-263 validation
  • Compliance evidence packages prepared per current DoD guidance
  • SRA Portal submission via DoD-approved ECA credentials
  • Government retains final compliance validation authority at all times
  • Supply chain traceability documentation maintained for all fulfilled parts
